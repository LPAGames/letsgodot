The Lore

In the year 2042, the digital and physical realms collided, creating Aetheria. It is a world powered by shards of pure data—NFTs that hold the essence of power. Players don't just play; they manifest. Every sword forged, every land claimed, and every victory earned is a unique asset living forever on the ledger.
