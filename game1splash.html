Class Spotlight

The Templar Paladin

In the heart of the Colosseum of Ash, the Templar stands as a monolith of unyielding faith. You do not just play a warrior; you become a vessel of divine kinetic force.

Every swing of your heavy mace carries the momentum of a falling star, designed to crush plate armor and shatter the resolve of colossal monsters and rival champions alike.

Steel & Sanctity

The Flanged Mace

Experience "Heavyweight Combat Syntax." Unlike blades that slice, your flanged mace interacts with physics. Strike a boss's limb to cause Fracture Debuffs, or overhead-smash another player to drain their stamina instantly. The impact creates a shockwave of holy light, punishing those who stand too close to your righteous fury.

The Great Bulwark Shield

Your shield is not just for defense—it is a weapon of displacement. Hold your ground against a screen-filling boss to trigger Perfect Reflection, turning their own kinetic energy back against them. In PvP, use the shield-edge to stagger opponents, opening a window for a crushing mace finisher.
