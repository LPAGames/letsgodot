The World of Eldoria

A thousand years after the Great Quenching, the embers of magic have begun to glow once more. In a land fractured by warring baronies and ancient curses, you are the last of the Ember-Born. Explore vast open landscapes, delve into forgotten dungeons, and rewrite the destiny of a dying world.

150+ Quests
40km² Open World
12 Unique Bosses

Game Features

Dynamic Combat

Master a deep, physics-based combat system where timing and positioning are as vital as your stats.

Living World

NPCs follow realistic schedules and the environment reacts to your choices throughout the story.

Alchemy & Crafting

Gather rare ingredients from dangerous peaks to brew powerful decoctions and forge legendary gear.

Elemental Magic

Harness the power of Fire, Frost, and Lightning. Combine elements to create devastating chain reactions.
